about DOF

Why DOF?


DOF (Digitalization of organic Food) endeavors to revolutionize the food industry by leveraging digital technologies to streamline food processing and sales. The primary goal is to champion natural, high-quality, and culturally significant food products. This initiative aims to combat prevalent challenges such as food adulteration, opacity in the food supply chain, public health issues, convenience, accessibility, cultural food preferences, and food security.

OBjective of DOF:

DOF is committed to revolutionizing the food industry by promoting natural, high-quality, and culturally relevant food products while addressing key challenges such as food adulteration, lack of transparency, health concerns, convenience, accessibility, cultural food preferences, and food security. Through DOF’s innovative approach and dedication to sustainability, it aims to create a more ethical and resilient food system that benefits both consumers and producers.

History of DOF:


 The idea to establish DOF was born out of Founder’s own experience. His family lives hundreds of kilometers away from where he live now, and he often struggle to find basic foods like Shiro, honey, butter, and berbere (spiced peppers), which are essential in Ethiopian food culture. As a result, he has to buy these items from nearby shops, but most of the time; these foods are adulterated, which has potential damage on health and test. Thus, the Owner, like many others do has had to rely on his family to prepare and provide him these foods, which can be quite expensive and exhausting. To address these challenges, He has decided to establish a company called DOF which is aimed at processing natural agricultural food and delivers it to users based on their food culture, using digital technology. Since its establishment DOF is working towards this goal for the past two years.

Issues the company seeks to address:
DOF seeks to address several key issues in the food industry, particularly in urban areas. Some of the main issues they aim to tackle include:

  1. Food Adulteration: DOF addresses the problem of food adulteration by providing a transparent and trustworthy supply chain for organic food products. By digitalizing the food processing and sales, they ensure that customers have access to natural, high-quality food without harmful additives or adulterants.
  2. Lack of Transparency: The company aims to bring transparency to the food supply chain by enabling customers to trace the origin and quality of the products they purchase. This transparency helps build trust between consumers and producers, leading to a more accountable and reliable food system.
  3. Health Concerns: DOF focuses on promoting healthier food options for urban residents by offering natural and organic products. By eliminating risks associated with processed and adulterated foods, they contribute to improving the overall health and well-being of their customers.
  4. Convenience and Accessibility: The company addresses the challenge of accessing natural and high-quality food products in urban areas by providing a digital platform that connects consumers with agricultural products. This convenience makes it easier for urban residents to access fresh, natural foods without having to compromise on quality.
  5. Cultural Food Preferences: DOF caters to diverse cultural food preferences by offering a wide range of natural agricultural products that appeal to different tastes and dietary requirements. This inclusivity ensures that all customers can find suitable options that align with their cultural backgrounds and personal preferences.
    6. Overall, DOF seeks to revolutionize the way consumers’ access and consume food by addressing key issues in the food industry and promoting a healthier, more transparent, and convenient food system for all.
